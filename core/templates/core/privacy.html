Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

The UJ Volunteer Portal ("we", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our students and staff. This policy outlines how we handle your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to manage volunteer activities and academic records:

  • Personal Details: Name, Surname, Student/Staff Number, and Email Address.
  • Activity Data: Volunteer hours logged, events attended, and campus affiliations.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Verifying student identity and academic standing.
  • Tracking community engagement hours for certification.
  • Generating aggregate reports for the University Community Engagement (UCE) division.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Data is only accessible by:

  • Portal Administrators: To manage the platform.
  • Coordinators: To verify attendance for events you RSVP to.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures including password encryption (hashing) and secure session management to protect your account.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about your data, please contact the UJ Community Engagement office.
