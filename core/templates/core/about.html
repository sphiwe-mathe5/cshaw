Connecting Campus & Community

The UJ Volunteer Portal bridges the gap between students and meaningful community engagement. We track impact, manage events, and empower students to make a difference.

For Students

Take charge of your volunteer journey.

  • Browse Events: Find opportunities at APB, DFC, APK, and SWC.
  • Track Hours: Automatically log your service hours.
  • Earn Badges: Get recognized for your contribution (Top Volunteer, Peer Educator).
  • History: View your full activity history for your CV.

For Coordinators

Simplify event management and reporting.

  • Create Events: Post new opportunities in seconds.
  • Digital Attendance: Say goodbye to paper. Manage RSVPs and attendance digitally.
  • Live Stats: Monitor how many spots are left in real-time.
  • Reports: Generate automated reports for university compliance.
